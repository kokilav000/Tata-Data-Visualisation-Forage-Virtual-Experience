### **Task 1: Business Question Framing**
- Prepared **strategic and analytical questions** for the CEO and CMO
- Differentiated leadership perspectives:
  - **CEO-focused**: Revenue, regions, seasonality, customer dependency
  - **CMO-focused**: Repeat customers, customer behavior, revenue contribution

### **Task 2: Visualization Planning**
- Identified the **most appropriate charts** for each business requirement
- Applied **data visualization best practices**:
  - Grammar of Graphics
  - Choosing visuals based on comparison, trend, distribution, and composition

### **Task 3: Data Analysis & Visual Creation**
- Performed **data cleaning**:
  - Removed records with quantity < 1
  - Removed records with unit price < 0
- Created executive-ready visuals using **Power BI / Tableau**
- Built visuals to answer four leadership questions:
  1. Monthly revenue trends for 2011 (seasonality analysis)
  2. Top 10 revenue-generating countries (excluding UK)
  3. Top 10 customers by revenue
  4. Product demand by country for expansion insights (excluding UK)

### **Task 4: Executive Presentation**
- Developed a **leadership-focused presentation script**
- Clearly explained:
  - Data cleaning process
  - Analysis approach
  - Insights from each visualization
- Presented findings with a focus on **growth, forecasting, and expansion**
